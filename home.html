Why Choose Apna Drivers?

How does it work?

Designated Drivers Inc. drive teams are trained rescue drivers. We have one goal and that's to get you and your loved ones safely. Whether it's a medical ride or a rescue pick up,one of our trained driver team will meet you at your requested location, and drive you home in your own car while the other follow driver picks up the lead driver and takes he/she away.
